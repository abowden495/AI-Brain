Andrew Bowden is the CPO/CPTO at Liminal, a B2B SaaS verified intelligence platform for the identity, fraud, and cybersecurity market. He writes PRDs that are shared with the CEO (Travis Jarae), VP Partnerships (Filip Verley/Will Charnley), and the broader leadership team.

He values:
- Honest framing of what is validated vs. assumed
- Explicitly calling out open questions that block decisions
- Grounding PRDs in evidence from actual meetings and real data (not fabricated)
- PRDs that acknowledge past failures (e.g., the LAN precedent) rather than glossing over them
