On 2026-03-14, completed deep market analysis of Liminal Networking App PRD v0.4.

**Key findings:**
- $53B/$429B professional networking market size is misleading (dominated by LinkedIn ad revenue); should be removed from PRD
- "No buyer-side community exists" claim is false -- CISO Society, CyberRisk Collaborative (3,000+ members), Evanta/Gartner CISO (3,600+), and RSAC year-round community all serve buyer-side needs
- Actual white space: no community exists for the SPECIFIC identity/fraud/cyber buyer persona with Liminal's trust density
- Hampton model works because of in-person 8-person groups, not digital platform -- PRD proposes digital-first
- Gartner "2x spending" stat could not be publicly verified; needs sourcing
- RSAC's 2025 rebrand to community platform is the most direct well-funded competitive threat
- Realistic member pool: 3,000-6,000 qualified individuals globally; 200-500 member club ceiling
- Tollgate model is well-designed; recommended T3 focus on small group facilitation not community feed

**Why:** Liminal is evaluating whether to invest in a curated networking product for identity/fraud/cyber buyers.
