## Liminal Strategic Context (as of 2026-03-12)

- Product: Verified intelligence platform for Identity, Fraud, and Cybersecurity market
- Stage: Post-PMF, growing user base
- Key asset: Proprietary Living Graph of 2.5M+ verified entities
- Two interfaces: Command (strategy) and GTM (revenue execution)
- Four agentic capabilities: Coach AI, Insights Agent, Target Account Lists, Team Spaces
- Enterprise customers: Visa, Mastercard, Google, JPMC

## Q1 2026 Strategic Priorities
1. Deepen enterprise account expansion (multi-team adoption)
2. Prove agentic capabilities deliver measurable customer outcomes
3. Accelerate Living Graph density as compounding moat

## Key Strategic Decisions
- Depth over breadth: deeper with fewer large accounts, not scaling account count yet
- No horizontal market expansion beyond Identity/Fraud/Cyber this quarter
- No self-serve/PLG motion -- high-touch Analyst Desk is a differentiator
- Prove existing 4 agentic features before building new ones

## North Star Metric
Time from market signal to customer action
