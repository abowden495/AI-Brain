## Networking App PRD v0.4 Review (2026-03-14)

**What was reviewed:** PRD for a curated networking app targeting enterprise buyers in Identity/Fraud/Cyber. Club model (200-500 members), seeded from LCS Summit attendees. Tollgate-gated investment approach.

**Key strategic findings:**
1. Gartner Peer Community analogy is flawed -- Gartner layers community on a $6B product; Liminal proposes building community to create a moat. Different strategic bets.
2. Flywheel is structurally identical to failed LAN thesis. Only 2 of 5 claimed differences are genuinely structural (value direction, data governance).
3. Monetization ambiguity (free vs paid) means no clear standalone success criterion.
4. Buyer App collision is under-examined -- both target same persona, same quarter, contradicts "depth over breadth" strategy.
5. "Real insider intelligence" content thesis is unproven for this security-conscious audience.

**Recommendations given:**
- Add Sean Ellis PMF question as hard gate at Tollgate 3
- Resolve Buyer App resource competition at Tollgate 1 (not deferred)
- Set hard 90-day sunset date on concierge phase
- Separate interview conductor from CEO/leadership to reduce relationship compliance bias in T2

**Why this matters:** Informs future strategy discussions about networking product and Buyer App prioritization.
