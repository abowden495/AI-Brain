# Product Information

**Product Name:** Liminal
**Stage:** PMF - Product-market fit achieved, growing user base
**Last Updated:** 2026-03-12

## Description

Liminal is a verified intelligence platform for the Identity, Fraud, and Cybersecurity market, built on a proprietary Living Graph mapping 2.5M+ verified entities. It delivers actionable intelligence through two interfaces — Command for strategic decision-making and GTM for revenue execution — powered by Grounded Context Engineering and validated by a human Analyst Desk. The platform now includes agentic capabilities: Coach AI for real-time sales coaching, Insights Agent for automated pattern detection, Target Account Lists for precision account targeting, and Team Spaces for collaborative intelligence workflows.

## Target Market

B2B enterprise — strategy leaders (CEOs, CSOs, CPOs) and revenue leaders (CROs, CMOs, VP Sales) at companies operating in Identity, Fraud, and Cybersecurity. Trusted by Visa, Mastercard, Google, JPMC, and other leading platforms in digital identity.

## Value Proposition

Liminal replaces unverified AI outputs, stale analyst reports, and disconnected point solutions with a single verified intelligence system — where strategy and execution run on the same source of truth, agentic intelligence surfaces patterns before they become obvious, and teams go from market signal to sales conversation in hours instead of months.

## Product Type

B2B SaaS Platform (Web app)
